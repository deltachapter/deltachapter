Why We Need Your Help

We're fundraising to create a brand new backyard that will serve as a perfect space for our fraternity's events, sports, and social gatherings. With your generous support, we’ll be able to create a comfortable and welcoming space that will serve both our current members and future generations.

Our Vision for the Backyard

Here’s a look at the exciting upgrades we plan to make:

  • New Fence – A beautiful, durable fence to define the space and provide privacy.
  • Grill Area – Paved area with new grills, chairs, a firepit, and a charming gazebo.
  • Outdoor Lighting – Ambient lighting that will bring the space to life during evening events.
  • Speakers – Surround sound for music and announcements during fraternity gatherings.
