Help Us Transform Our Backyard!

Why We Need Your Help

We're fundraising to create a brand new backyard that will serve as a perfect space for our fraternity's brotherhood, alumni, and parent events. With your generous support, we’ll be able to create a comfortable and welcoming space for current members and future generations.

Our Vision for the Backyard

Here’s a look at the exciting upgrades we plan to make:

  • New Fence – A beautiful, durable fence to define the space and provide privacy, security, and safety.
  • Grill Area – A space with new grills, chairs, a firepit, and a charming gazebo for alumni and parent gatherings.
  • Outdoor Lighting – Ambient lighting that will provide the brothers with safety at night.
  • Speakers – A surround sound system to make announcements on during alumni and parent events.

Backyard Overview

Key:

Brown Border Around House: This is where the fence is going to go.

Yellow Dots on the Fence: This is where we plan on putting in lights to keep the entire backyard lit.

Black Dots on the Fence: This is where the permanent speakers are going to go.

Red and Orange Circle in the Upper Right Hand Side of the Fenced in Area: This is the new Grill Area.
